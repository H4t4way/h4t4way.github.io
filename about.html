About me

My name is Reando, but you can call me H4t4way. I am a penetration tester and occasional bug hunter.

As a lover of WebSec, I always like to find something wrong and fix it !!!! :) I have been passionate about computer science since childhood and have been in the security world for five and a half years

I started studying IT many years ago in school and university where I learned JAVA and C and during my workingcareer PHP, Python, Javascript.

Main Activities:

  • Web Application Testing ASVS level 1,2,3
  • Application Security
  • Web Application DAST, SAST, OSS scans
  • Network Penetration Testing
  • Learning Blockchain Security

In the last 6 months focusing on Blockchain Security, learning Solidity and Rust. Sometimes I also take part in CTFs.

I'm founder of Pentesting Made Simple an Italian community where we speak about Pentesting, Bug Bounty and Ethical Hacking in general.
