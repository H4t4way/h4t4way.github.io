About Me

Hello everyone!
I'm H4t4way. I am a penetration tester, security researcher and an occasional bug hunter.
As a lover of WebSec, I always like to find something wrong and fix it !!!! :)
I have been passionate about computer science since childhood and have been in the security world for five and a half years.
